Do you want to learn how to make your favorite desserts?

    Welcome to my page! If you want to learn how to start making desserts for yourself or your loved ones, you are in the right spot!

    Nearly 10 years ago, "Tasty", now-renowned food channel, made their first video on how to make simple desserts. Over the years, they have posted more videos about food, desserts, and even challenges related to them. Being run by the company, Buzzfeed, they were very quick to grow into a huge channel that now has 21.3M subscribers. Although I don't have the skills to bake, I loved watching their aesthetically satisfying videos and became a big fan. This website was created to be an easier way to store the recipes of all the creative desserts we all love.
