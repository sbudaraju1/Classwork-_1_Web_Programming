Cookies

    Here is the recipe for the best chocolate chip cookies!

    1. Sift 1 cup of bread flour, 3/4 cup all-purpose flour, 2 tsp salt, and 1 tsp baking soda in a bowl.
    2. Make 1 cup melted browned butter.
      • To make browned butter, melt it in a pot and let it boil for a few minutes. Continously stir for even browning and once there are brown bits, pour it into a measuring cup. This allows for it to stop cooking and not burn.
      • If needed, add water to make it 1 cup.
      • Set aside to cool to room temperature.
    3. Take another bowl and add 1 cup dark brown sugar, 1/2 cup white sugar, 2 tsp vanilla extract, and 1 tsp espresso powder.
    4. Add the cooled butter.
    5. Add 1 full egg and 1 egg-yolk.
    6. Mix these together and slowly start to incorporate the flour.
    7. Mix in 1/2 cup of semisweet chocolate chips and 5 oz of dark chocolate chunks.
    8. Chill overnight in the fridge for best flavor (or chill for 1 hour).
    9. Bake for 12-14 minutes at 350 F (180 C).
